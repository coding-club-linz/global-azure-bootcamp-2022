Azure and Cloud Computing Conference

Austrian Edition of Global Azure 2022

Global Azure is a 3-day virtual conference in which communities around the world organize live streams to learn about Cloud Computing and Microsoft Azure. This is the Austrian edition of Global Azure 2022 provided by the Coding Club Linz.

We Are Virtual!

The current Covid-situation does not allow us to invite you all to Linz (Austria). Like last year, Global Azure 2022 will be a virtual event again. You can watch all sessions on YouTube, ask questions in comments, and join live discussions on Discord.

Everything Live!

At Global Azure Austria, all sessions including live demos will be performed live. Therefore, speakers can answer questions and interact with the audience during their talks.

German and English Content

The majority of our sessions will be in German. However, in order to offer content for a broad audience, some speakers have agreed to deliver their sessions in English so that people who do not understand German fluently can participate in the event, too.

Join us on Friday, May 6, 2022!

Speakers

Internationally highly respected speakers will share their knowledge about topics around Azure and Cloud Computing.

Sponsors

Thanks to the generous support of our great sponsors the event will be free for everybody.
