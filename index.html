Azure and Cloud Computing Conference

Austrian Edition of Global Azure 2025

Global Azure is a 3-day virtual conference in which communities around the world organize events to learn about Cloud Computing and Microsoft Azure. This is the Austrian edition of Global Azure 2025 provided by the Coding Club Linz.

Global Azure Austria is from the community, for the community and thanks to our great sponsors free for everybody.

We Are Hybrid, and there is great food!

After some years as a virtual conference, 2025 will be a hybrid event. You can join us in person at the Hollywood Megaplex PlusCity or watch sessions in the live stream.

Vegetarian food (vegan options will be available, too) and drinks for all registered attendees will be provided. A big thank you goes to our sponsors for making free food and drinks possible!

Everything Live!

As a hybrid event, we have speakers on-site and remote. All sessions - including the remote ones - will be live and interactive. You can ask questions and discuss with the speakers and other attendees.

German and English Content

In order to offer content for a broad audience, we have sessions delivered in German and sessions in English so that people who do not understand German fluently can participate in the event, too.

Join us on Saturday, May 10th, 2025!

Build Their Platform, Not Yours: Empowering Developers

Saturday, April 20, 2024 11:55 - 12:40 CEST

Developer platforms are designed to make developers' lives easier. When built with the end user in mind, they can streamline workflows, foster collaboration, and speed up innovation. In essence, they act as a helpful assistant that takes care of the complex behind-the-scenes work, freeing developers to focus on what they usually do best ‚Äì building great software.

This naturally leads us to some critical questions: How much of the underlying complexity should these platforms abstract away? How can we empower developers with helpful tools without making their work feel rigid? And how can we cater to the diverse needs and preferences within development teams?

This talk will explore various approaches to crafting opinionated user interfaces and compare their strengths and weaknesses. We'll journey from the concept of the thinnest viable platform (TVP) to the pitfalls of over-engineering, where meaningful abstractions turn into limitations.
