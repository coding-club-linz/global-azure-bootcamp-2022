Azure and Cloud Computing Conference

Austrian Edition of Global Azure 2024

Global Azure is a 3-day virtual conference in which communities around the world organize events to learn about Cloud Computing and Microsoft Azure. This is the Austrian edition of Global Azure 2024 provided by the Coding Club Linz.

Global Azure Austria is from the community, for the community and thanks to our great sponsors free for everybody.

We Are Hybrid, and there is great food!

After some years as a virtual conference, 2024 will be a hybrid event. You can join us in person at the HTBLA Leonding or watch sessions in the live stream.

Vegetarian food (vegan options will be available, too) and drinks for all registered attendees will be provided by Good Karma Gastro. A big thank you goes to our sponsors for making free food and drinks possible!

Everything Live!

As a hybrid event, we have speakers on-site and remote. All sessions - including the remote ones - will be live and interactive. You can ask questions and discuss with the speakers and other attendees.

German and English Content

In order to offer content for a broad audience, we have sessions delivered in German and sessions in English so that people who do not understand German fluently can participate in the event, too.

Join us on Saturday, April 20th, 2024!
