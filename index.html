Azure and Cloud Computing Conference

Austrian Edition of Global Azure 2024

Global Azure is a 3-day virtual conference in which communities around the world organize live streams to learn about Cloud Computing and Microsoft Azure. This is the Austrian edition of Global Azure 2024 provided by the Coding Club Linz.

We Are Hybrid!

After some years as a virtual conference, 2024 will be a hybrid event. You can join us in-person at the [HTBLA Leonding](https://www.htl-leonding.at/) or watch session in the live stream.

Everything Live!

As a hybrid event, we have speakers on-site and remote. All sessions - including the remote ones - will be live and interactive. You can ask questions and discuss with the speakers and other attendees.

German and English Content

In order to offer content for a broad audience, we have sessions delivered in German and sessions in English so that people who do not understand German fluently can participate in the event, too.

Join us on Saturday, April 20th, 2024!

Session Content

We are currently working on putting together the session schedule for the event. Reserve the date (April 20th, 2024) and subscribe to our mailing list (see form at the bottom of the page) to get notified when the schedule is available.

Speakers

Internationally highly respected speakers will share their knowledge about topics around Azure and Cloud Computing.
